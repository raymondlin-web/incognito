You've gone incognito

Now you can browse "privately", and other people who use this device won't see your activity to an extent. However, downloads and bookmarks and just about all your data will be saved. Learn more

Chrome won't save the following information:

  • Your browsing history

  • Websites you visit
  • Your employer or school

  • Parental controls and keylogging systems
  • Those annoying people looking over your shoulder
